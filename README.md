## 🚀 Featured Projects

### ⭐ [EMERgency Aid Delivery Hexacopter](https://github.com/Ox0sama)
> A fully autonomous 550mm hexacopter designed to locate and deliver 2 lb emergency aid kits to victims in open public spaces (parks, plazas, streets). Simulated end-to-end in ROS 2 + Gazebo with grid lawnmower scanning, camera-based victim detection, servo payload release, and 30 mph wind gust tolerance. Frame designed in OnShape; flight stack powered by Pixhawk 4 with MAVLink integration.
